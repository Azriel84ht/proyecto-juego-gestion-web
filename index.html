Grave New World

A spooky, cartoonish adventure of alchemy and terror!

About the Game

Welcome to the world of Grave New World, where spooky cartoons and dark humor collide! In this game, you'll delve into the mysterious arts of alchemy, brew concoctions with blood, and encounter a host of terrifying (and funny) creatures.

Meet the Creatures

Grumble-snout

A grumpy creature with a penchant for shiny objects.

Shadow-lurker

This creature is shy, but its shadow has a mind of its own.

Giggle-goo

A gelatinous blob that communicates through laughter.

The Art of Alchemy

Alchemy is at the heart of Grave New World. Collect ingredients, experiment with recipes, and create powerful potions and monstrous creations. But be warned, not all experiments go as planned!
